Learn how to react

Don't feel afraid about doing something wrong, the most important is to do something.
Did you know that the victim of a heart attack can die in 10 minutes, and that the survival rate falls by 10% for every minute? That is why the cardiac massage and the use of a defibrillator are crucial for the resuscitation of the patient.

Step 1 : Call emergency services

Keep calm, and call the emergency medical service as soon as possible. They will help and guide you to handle the situation at best, and send a team to take over.

Step 2 : Check that the victim is unresponsive

Only use a defibrillator if the patient does not respond. Otherwise, wait for the medical services to arrive on site.

Step 3 : Prepare the patient

Remove clothes from the patient's upper body. Do not hesitate to cut them if necessary.

Step 4 : Apply the electrodes

Apply the electrodes on patient's bare chest as shown on diagram. One electrode is placed on the right, below the clavicle. Another electrode is applied to the left side of the patient, just below and to the left of the pectoral muscle.

Step 5 : Wait for heart rhythm analysis

Do not touch the victim, and wait for the defibrillator to analyse heart rhythm. The heart rythm analysis will starts without any intervention. Do not worry, the defibrillator will detect fibrilaltion and only shock if necessary.

Step 6 : Shock advised

Before a shock delivery, the defibrillator will warn you to be careful not to touch the patient. In semi-automatic mode, the device informs you to deliver the shock by pressing the "shock" button. In automatic mode, it alerts you that the shock will be delivered without your intervention.

Step 7 : Cardiac massage

Each shock must be followed by cardiac massage. The device will inform you when and how to make a chest compression. A metronome will guide you to perform cardiac massage in the right rhythm. Once the cardiac massage is finished, the survival cycle is repeated, until an ambulance arrives on site.

Step 8 : Treatment of the victim

Once ambulance paramedics arrive on site, they will start the treatment of the victim. Congratulations, you may have saved a life!
