You are around when someone has sudden cardiac arrest, or you want , you can follow a few simple steps to use a defibrillator and life-saving techniques.
Confirm cardiac arrest Airway: You need to make sure the airway is open before you check his breathing. To do this, tilt back his head and lift up his chin.[3] If you see an object obstructing the airway, remove it. Breathing: Lean in closely to listen for breathing. Look to see if his chest is rising and falling.[4] Circulation: Feel for a pulse. Signs of circulatory issues include color changes, sweating, and a lower level of consciousness.[5] Call emergency services Explain to them where you are and what is going on. If there is someone else there other than you, make them call 911 while you start working on the person in need
